# The Olive Branch Network Charter

*Status: Draft Charter v0.1 — Frozen pending implementation validation. See Article X.*

**Article I — Purpose.** Olive Branch Network exists to make it possible for a
person to preserve ownership of their assets while continuously directing a
portion of the value those assets generate toward independently accountable
public benefit.

**Article II — Consent.** Participation in Olive Branch Network is voluntary.
The protocol may enable a Participant to direct value toward public benefit;
it may never compel, default, or assume that direction without the
Participant's affirmative and revocable choice.

**Article III — Separability.** Ownership of an asset and the direction of the
value it generates are independent. A Participant retains the former
permanently and controls the latter continuously.

**Article IV — Accountability.** Value may be directed only toward a
recipient whose legitimacy as public benefit is independently verifiable.
Self-declared legitimacy is not sufficient.

**Article V — Commons.** Value generated but claimed by no individual
Designation is governed by the collective decision of the Participants who
generated it, not by the operator of the protocol.

**Article VI — Verifiability.** Every Designation, Contribution, and
Governance decision must be permanently recorded and independently checkable
by any party.

**Article VII — Definitions.** The following terms are fixed for use
throughout every document, system, and interface derived from this Charter:
**Participant** — an owner exercising the capability in Article I. **Public
Benefit** — a verified destination for directed value. **Designation** — a
Participant's continuous, revocable instruction for where their generated
value flows. **Contribution** — a realized instance of a Designation.
**Governance** — the collective process resolving what no Designation claims.
**Record** — the permanent, independently checkable account of all of the
above.

**Article VIII — The Constraint.** Any future feature must:
1. Obtain affirmative, revocable consent before creating or modifying a
   Designation.
2. Preserve the Participant's ownership of their underlying asset.
3. Direct the value that asset generates toward a verified instance of Public
   Benefit.
4. Verify that any recipient is independently accountable, not self-declared.
5. Respect collective Governance over any value no Designation claims.
6. Record the action permanently and independently of any single party.

A feature failing any clause does not belong in Olive Branch Network.

**Article IX — Scope of Binding Force.** This Charter does not bind: the
chain OBN operates on; the token standard; the staking mechanism; the
structure and cadence of governance cycles; the current scope of recognized
Public Benefit, presently limited to publicly registered nonprofit
organizations; or the database, API, MCP tools, and smart contracts
implementing the Articles above. These may be changed by ordinary governance
or engineering decision at any time.

**Article X — Amendment.** This Charter may be amended only through the
collective governance process required by Article V. No single party,
including the protocol's operators, may amend it unilaterally.
